# 7-Day Plan — Survivor (Destiny’s Child)

## Day 1 — Mapping + baseline take
- Mark form, breath spots, hard lines
- Record one baseline run (no pressure)

## Day 2 — Groove + diction day
- Rhythm speak-through with metronome
- Verse clarity drills + one clean verse recording

## Day 3 — Chorus mechanics
- Chorus stamina drills
- 3-pass intensity ladder (70/85/100)

## Day 4 — Vowel shaping + mix stability
- Sustain-word work (survivor/stronger/fighter)
- Round/bright balance on higher phrases

## Day 5 — Full integration
- Full song at 85% performance energy
- Fix top 2 remaining weak spots only

## Day 6 — Performance simulation
- Full one-take run as if live
- Immediate notes: pitch, breath, clarity, impact

## Day 7 — Lock + review
- Final clean take
- Note what to maintain next week (3 keeps, 2 tweaks)
