# Black Atlas Productions — Internal Operator Strategy Pack (Execution v1)

## Operator Objective
Build a repeatable production business with disciplined execution, predictable revenue development, and high-quality artistic output.

## 12-Month Top Outcomes
1. Deliver 3 Black Atlas Live events at quality standard
2. Secure baseline sponsor + grant + ticket revenue mix
3. Implement reliable operating system (planning, finance, reporting)
4. Exit Year 1 with Season Two launch readiness

## Strategic Priorities
### Priority 1: Revenue Engine
- Stand up sponsor pipeline and outreach cadence
- Submit targeted grant applications
- Build presale and ticket conversion mechanics

### Priority 2: Production System
- Standardize run-of-show and role assignments
- Create reusable vendor/venue process templates
- Keep documented post-event debrief loop

### Priority 3: Brand and Audience Growth
- Publish consistent pre/post event content
- Capture proof assets each cycle (photo, video, testimonials)
- Grow email and direct audience channels

### Priority 4: Financial Discipline
- Weekly cash-flow visibility
- Line-item budget ownership by category
- Scenario planning: base / conservative / stretch

## Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday (60 min):** strategy + pipeline planning
- **Tuesday/Wednesday:** outreach + production execution blocks
- **Thursday:** finance/admin + sponsor follow-up
- **Friday (45 min):** review KPIs, blockers, next-week top 3

## KPI Dashboard (Minimum)
### Revenue KPIs
- Pipeline value (sponsors)
- Sponsor meetings/week
- Proposal sends/week
- Closed revenue/month

### Program KPIs
- Event readiness score
- Attendance and conversion
- Artist/crew payout totals
- On-time deliverables rate

### Brand KPIs
- Email list growth
- Social engagement rate
- Partner retention rate

## Decision Rules
Before saying yes to a project/opportunity, confirm:
1. Does it align with Black Atlas brand thesis?
2. Does it create measurable value (revenue, impact, infrastructure)?
3. Can we execute without quality dilution?

If 2+ answers are no, do not proceed.

## Immediate 30-Day Execution List
1. Finalize sponsor deck and tier matrix
2. Build first 30 qualified sponsor prospects
3. Lock top 3 grant opportunities + deadlines
4. Finalize event operations template stack
5. Launch KPI tracker and weekly reviews

## Operating Standard
We are building a business, not just producing events. Every cycle must leave us with stronger systems, better data, and clearer leverage for the next cycle.
