# Black Atlas Brand Workspace

## Folders
- `references/` moodboards and inspiration captures
- `kit/` core specs (palette, type, boilerplate, logo rules)
- `deck/` working slide files and cover variants
- `one-sheets/` sponsor and grant one-sheet templates
- `social/` social tile templates and exports
- `templates/` reusable source templates
- `exports/` final output bundles (PDF/PNG)
