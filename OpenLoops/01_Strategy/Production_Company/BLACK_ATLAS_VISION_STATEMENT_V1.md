# Black Atlas Productions — Vision Statement v1

## Core Vision (3-Year)
Black Atlas Productions will become Wichita’s most respected independent creative production house for bold, high-craft live events, stage work, and screen projects—known for unforgettable audience experiences and a reliable pipeline of paid opportunities for local artists.

## Mission
We create gritty, original, multidisciplinary work that leaves people inspired, changed, and moved.

## Creative Identity
- Mythic + modern storytelling
- Fashion-forward visual sensibility
- Emotional impact over noise
- Professional execution and artistic risk

## What Black Atlas Is Building
1. A repeatable production engine (not one-off events)
2. A trusted local artist network with paid pathways
3. A recognizable cultural brand with regional pull
4. A portfolio that ladders from live events -> stage -> film

## What Black Atlas Is Not
- Not a random event promoter with no artistic thesis
- Not unpaid-labor dependent
- Not overstuffed with low-quality output
- Not chasing every opportunity at the expense of brand coherence

## Strategic Positioning
Black Atlas sits between independent artistry and premium production execution: culturally bold, operationally disciplined.

## 3-Year Outcomes We’re Aiming For
- 2–4 annual flagship live productions with recurring audiences
- A stable sponsor + grant + earned-revenue mix
- A documented roster of returning artists/crew
- A pipeline of stage/film projects seeded from live programming
- Regional recognition as a high-quality independent production brand

## Guiding Principles
- Pay artists whenever possible, transparently
- Protect quality and story integrity
- Build systems that reduce chaos and increase consistency
- Measure impact with both numbers and narrative evidence
- Leave every project with reusable assets (media, process, partnerships)
