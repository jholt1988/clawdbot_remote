# Black Atlas Productions — Grant Narrative (Longform Draft)

## Organizational Background
Black Atlas Productions is a Wichita-based independent production company creating cross-disciplinary work across live events, stage, and film. Our brand language, *Beautiful trouble, expertly made*, reflects a commitment to artistic risk and technical rigor. We are building a repeatable local production model that supports artists with paid opportunities and audiences with original, high-quality cultural experiences.

## Statement of Need
Wichita has substantial artistic talent, but independent creators often lack consistent paid opportunities, production infrastructure, and visibility pathways. Existing opportunities are often project-by-project and not structured for long-term career growth. Black Atlas Live is designed to close this gap by combining paid artist labor, professional event execution, and documentation assets that advance artist portfolios and future work.

## Program Design
Season One of Black Atlas Live will deliver three large-scale 18+ events in Wichita. Each event will feature multidisciplinary performance programming and a consistent production standard, including technical support, marketing activation, and documentation.

### Core program components
- Paid artist and crew contracts
- Venue and production operations
- Cohesive creative direction across all three events
- Documentation package per event (photo + short highlight)
- Post-event evaluation and reporting

## Population Served
Primary beneficiaries are Wichita-based artists and early-career creatives in music, performance, theatre-adjacent, design, and technical production roles. Secondary beneficiaries include local audiences and venue partners.

## Goals and Outcomes
### Output goals
- 3 completed events
- 45 paid artist/crew roles
- 900 attendees total
- 3 recurring venue/community partners

### Outcome goals
- 75%+ audience impact score (inspired/changed/moved)
- 100% artist documentation delivery and project crediting
- Demonstrated repeatable operating model for Season Two

## Evaluation Plan
We will use short audience surveys, artist/crew post-event forms, attendance and compensation records, and partner feedback. Data will be compiled after each event and summarized in a season-end report including quantitative outcomes and qualitative narrative evidence.

## Sustainability
Black Atlas Live is intentionally structured as a blended-revenue model: grants + sponsorship + ticket income + in-kind support. Grant funding de-risks first-season launch while we build recurring sponsorship relationships, stronger earned revenue, and long-term partner alignment.
