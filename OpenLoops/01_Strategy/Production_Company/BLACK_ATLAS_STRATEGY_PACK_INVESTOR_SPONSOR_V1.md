# Black Atlas Productions — Investor/Sponsor Strategy Pack (Concise v1)

## What Black Atlas Is
Black Atlas Productions is a Wichita-based independent production company building a premium local cultural brand through high-impact live events, then expanding into stage and film.

## Why It Wins
- Clear creative positioning (*Beautiful trouble, expertly made.*)
- Repeatable operating model (not one-off events)
- Revenue diversification (sponsorship + tickets + grants)
- Strong brand documentation flywheel (content -> audience growth -> sponsor value)

## Year 1 Product
**Black Atlas Live: Season One**
- 3 large-scale 18+ events in Wichita
- Paid artist + crew opportunities
- Full recap media package per event

## Revenue Model (Year 1)
- Sponsorships/donations
- Ticket sales
- Grants
- Select production services

## Sample Unit Economics (Season One)
- Total budget: ~$18,000
- 3 events (~$6,000/event)
- Target attendance: ~900 cumulative

## Sponsor Value Proposition
Sponsors get culturally credible brand alignment plus measurable outcomes:
- On-site and digital brand visibility
- Activation opportunities
- Audience engagement data
- Recap content and post-season reporting

## Key KPIs
- Attendance and ticket conversion
- Sponsor pipeline and close rate
- Audience impact score
- Partner retention and repeat participation

## Capital/Support Use
- Artist and crew compensation
- Venue and technical production quality
- Marketing and audience development
- Documentation/reporting infrastructure

## 12-Month Milestones
- Q1: sponsor/grant pipeline setup + asset completion
- Q2: first commitments + launch marketing
- Q3: execute events 1 & 2
- Q4: execute event 3 + season report + expansion prep

## Ask
Partner with Black Atlas as a sponsor/investor-aligned supporter to help build a durable creative production platform in Wichita with measurable artistic and community impact.
