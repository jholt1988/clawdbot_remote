# Black Atlas Productions — Grant Funder Strategy Pack (v1)

## Organization
Black Atlas Productions is a Wichita-based independent production company creating multidisciplinary work across live events, stage, and film. Our mission is to produce bold, high-quality work that leaves audiences inspired, changed, and moved while expanding paid pathways for local artists.

## Community Need
Wichita creatives face limited access to:
1. Consistent paid production opportunities
2. Professional-caliber independent platforms
3. Documented visibility that supports career advancement

Black Atlas Live addresses these gaps through structured local production, artist compensation, and measurable audience/community impact.

## Funded Program
### Black Atlas Live — Season One (18+)
- 3 major events in Wichita
- Paid contracts for performers and crew
- Professional production and documentation per event
- Built-in evaluation and reporting

## Beneficiaries
**Primary:** Wichita-based artists and early-career creatives (performers, technicians, designers).  
**Secondary:** local audiences, venues, and partner organizations.

## Outcomes Framework
### Outputs
- 3 completed events
- 45 paid artist/crew roles
- 900 attendees total
- 3+ active local partners

### Outcomes
- 75%+ audience impact response (inspired/changed/moved)
- 100% artist documentation/credit delivery
- Repeatable operating model and Season Two readiness

## Equity, Access, and Safety
- Transparent compensation practices
- Respectful production environment standards
- Budget-dependent access pathways (reduced-price/community allocations)
- Clear 18+ policies and venue compliance

## Evaluation Plan
- Audience survey collection at each event
- Artist/crew post-event outcomes surveys
- Attendance and compensation records
- Partner feedback capture
- Season-end impact report (quantitative + qualitative)

## Sustainability Plan
Year 1 uses a blended model:
- Grants (launch stabilization)
- Sponsorships/donations
- Earned revenue (ticket sales)
- In-kind support

Grant funding enables program launch and infrastructure development while earned/sponsor income scales in subsequent cycles.

## Budget Snapshot
- Season One budget target: ~$18,000
- Largest allocations: artist pay, technical production, venue, marketing, documentation

## Funding Request
Black Atlas requests grant support to launch and execute Season One of Black Atlas Live as a repeatable, community-rooted production model that grows paid artistic opportunity and cultural vitality in Wichita.
