## Slide 2 — Mission + Vision
### Mission
Black Atlas Productions creates and produces bold, independent work across live events, stage, and film that leaves audiences inspired, changed, and moved.

### Vision
Build a repeatable Wichita-based production platform that expands paid artistic opportunity and strengthens local cultural infrastructure.

- Limited high-quality independent production platforms
