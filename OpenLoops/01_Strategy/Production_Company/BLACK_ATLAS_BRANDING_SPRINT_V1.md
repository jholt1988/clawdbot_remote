# Black Atlas Productions — Branding Sprint (Start Here)

## Objective
Define a coherent, premium, gritty brand system that is usable across deck, web, social, event collateral, and sponsor materials.

## 1) Brand Core Decisions
- Brand promise (1 sentence)
- Brand personality (3–5 adjectives)
- Audience feeling target (before vs after experience)
- Brand boundaries (what we never look/sound like)

## 2) Visual Direction
- Primary palette (3 colors)
- Support neutrals (2–3)
- Typography pair (display + body)
- Photography direction (mood, contrast, composition)
- Graphic motifs (textures, symbols, framing)

## 3) Verbal Identity
- Voice traits (e.g., bold, refined, precise)
- Messaging do/don’t list
- Tagline usage rules
- Short boilerplate (50/100/150 words)

## 4) Brand System Outputs (Week 1)
- Logo usage sheet (or temporary wordmark rules)
- Color + type spec sheet
- 6 sample social tiles
- Deck cover + section slide style
- One-page brand guide v1

## 5) Fast Decisions Needed From Jordan
1. Should the brand read more **luxury underground** or **raw independent**?
2. Is the dominant emotional tone **seductive**, **defiant**, or **mythic**?
3. Do we prefer typography that feels **fashion editorial** or **cinematic bold**?
4. Are we leaning monochrome + one accent, or richer palette?
5. Keep tagline as-is, or test alternatives?

## 6) Immediate Next Move
Answer the 5 questions above, then we generate:
- Brand Direction Option A / B / C
- Final pick
- One-page brand guide v1
