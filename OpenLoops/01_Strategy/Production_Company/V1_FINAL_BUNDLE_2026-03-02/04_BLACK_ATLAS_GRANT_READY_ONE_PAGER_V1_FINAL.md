# Black Atlas Productions — Grant-Ready One-Pager

**Organization:** Black Atlas Productions  
**Location:** Wichita, Kansas  
**Program:** Black Atlas Live — Season One (18+)  
**Tagline:** *Beautiful trouble, expertly made.*

## Mission
Black Atlas Productions creates and produces gritty, independent work across film, stage, and live events that leaves audiences inspired, changed, and moved. Through Black Atlas Live, we present artist-forward, craft-driven 18+ performance events rooted in Wichita that blend bold storytelling, strong visual identity, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.

## Need / Opportunity
Wichita has deep creative talent, but limited repeatable platforms that combine:
1. **Paid opportunities** for local artists and production workers
2. **High production value** independent events
3. **Consistent documentation and visibility** that supports long-term artist growth

Black Atlas Live addresses this gap by creating a sustainable local production model and delivering original cultural experiences that strengthen Wichita’s creative ecosystem.

## Program Summary (Season One)
Black Atlas Live will produce **three (3) large-scale, 18+ ticketed events** in Wichita under a unified creative direction.

Each event includes:
- Curated multidisciplinary programming (music/performance/theatrical elements)
- Paid performer and crew contracts
- Professional event production and audience experience design
- Documentation package (photo + short highlight video)

## Participants & Beneficiaries
**Primary:** Wichita-based performers and creators (musicians, actors, dancers, vocalists, designers, technicians), especially early-career and independent artists.  
**Secondary:** Wichita audiences, venue partners, and local creative businesses through increased programming and foot traffic.

## 12-Month Timeline
- **Months 1–2:** finalize venue partners, dates, creative direction, artist outreach
- **Months 3–5:** development/rehearsals, production planning, marketing launch
- **Months 6–10:** execute all 3 events (staggered for quality and audience-building)
- **Months 11–12:** evaluation/reporting, documentation archive, Season Two planning

## Measurable Outcomes
### Outputs
- Events produced: **3**
- Paid artist/crew roles: **45 total** (avg. 15 per event)
- Total compensation distributed: **$9,000+**
- Attendance: **900 total** (avg. 300 per event)

### Impact
- **Audience impact:** 75%+ of surveyed attendees report feeling inspired/changed/moved
- **Artist outcomes:** 100% of participating artists receive portfolio-ready documentation and project credits
- **Ecosystem outcomes:** at least 3 recurring local partners (venues/vendors/media)

## Equity, Access, and Safety Commitments
- Transparent artist pay ranges and role expectations
- Respectful, professional rehearsal and event environments
- At least one access pathway per season (reduced-price allotment, comps through partners, or community allocations)
- Clear 18+ audience standards and venue compliance

## Organizational Readiness
- Core producing leadership in place
- Standardized run-of-show, contracting, and vendor workflows
- Documentation and post-event reporting process established
- Financial tracking via line-item budget + invoice/payment records

## Funding Request
Black Atlas Productions seeks grant support to launch and execute Season One of Black Atlas Live, establishing a repeatable production model that expands paid opportunities for Wichita artists and delivers high-impact local cultural experiences.
