# Black Atlas Productions — 90-Day Action Plan v1

## 90-Day Objective
Lock vision, validate business model, and build the minimum operating system needed to execute Season One of Black Atlas Live with confidence.

## Success Criteria by Day 90
- Vision and business plan finalized (v1.0)
- Sponsorship system live (deck + agreement + outreach list)
- Grant package ready for submission
- Season 1 production dates and partner path identified
- KPI dashboard and weekly operating cadence active

---

## Phase 1 (Days 1–30): Foundation + Positioning
### Priorities
1. Finalize vision, positioning, and offer architecture
2. Confirm Season 1 concept and event model
3. Build sponsor + grant core materials

### Deliverables
- Vision Statement v1.0
- Business Plan v1.0
- Grant one-pager + longform narrative draft
- Sponsorship agreement + tier matrix
- Sponsor deck outline

### Weekly KPI Targets
- 5 qualified sponsor prospects added/week
- 2 strategic partner conversations/week
- 1 grant opportunity identified/week

---

## Phase 2 (Days 31–60): Pipeline + Commitments
### Priorities
1. Activate sponsor outreach
2. Build venue and partner commitments
3. Finalize rough season budget and production assumptions

### Deliverables
- 30-prospect sponsor pipeline list
- Venue shortlist + hold strategy
- Season budget (base/conservative/stretch)
- Event staffing model draft

### Weekly KPI Targets
- 8 sponsor outreaches/week
- 3 sponsor conversations/week
- 1 proposal sent/week

---

## Phase 3 (Days 61–90): Execution Readiness
### Priorities
1. Convert commitments into execution timeline
2. Finalize first event launch workflow
3. Stand up reporting/evaluation infrastructure

### Deliverables
- Event #1 launch plan and run-of-show template
- Documentation plan (photo/video/reporting)
- Audience + artist survey forms
- KPI dashboard v1 (finance, pipeline, attendance targets)

### Weekly KPI Targets
- 1 signed sponsor/partner commitment per 2 weeks
- 1 completed grant submission every 2–3 weeks
- 1 operating system improvement documented/week

---

## Weekly Operating Rhythm (Non-Negotiable)
- **Monday:** strategy + pipeline planning (60 min)
- **Midweek:** outreach block + follow-up block
- **Friday:** weekly review (wins, blockers, next 3 priorities)

## Time Allocation (suggested)
- 40% revenue development (sponsors/grants/tickets)
- 30% production and operations
- 20% brand/marketing/content
- 10% admin/reporting

## Immediate Next 5 Moves (Start This Week)
1. Finalize and approve Vision Statement v1 and Business Plan v1
2. Build sponsor target list (first 30 prospects)
3. Convert sponsor deck outline into presentation draft
4. Select top 3 grant opportunities and map deadlines
5. Create one-page weekly KPI tracker and begin logging
