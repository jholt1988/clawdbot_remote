# Black Atlas Productions — One-Page Brand Guide v1

## Brand Core
**Positioning:** Raw independent production company with mythic ambition and editorial-level finish.  
**Mission:** Create bold, original work that leaves audiences inspired, changed, and moved.

## Chosen Direction
**Primary Direction:** Ritual Noir  
**Primary Tagline:** *Ritual, risk, and remarkable craft.*

## Voice & Tone
### Voice Traits
- Bold
- Precise
- Cultured
- Grounded (never pretentious)
- Emotionally charged, never chaotic

### Writing Rules
- Short, decisive sentences
- High signal, low fluff
- Evocative but clear
- No corporate buzzwords

### Avoid
- Overexplaining the art
- Generic “creative agency” language
- Hype without proof

## Visual System (v1)
### Palette (Monochrome + Accent)
- **Black:** `#0B0B0B`
- **Off-White:** `#F2EFEA`
- **Graphite:** `#2A2A2A`
- **Accent (Oxblood):** `#6E0F1E`

### Typography
- **Headline (fashion editorial serif):** Playfair Display / Canela-style equivalent
- **Body (clean sans):** Inter / Neue Haas Grotesk-style equivalent

### Layout Principles
- Strong negative space
- High contrast blocks
- Tight hierarchy (headline -> subhead -> proof)
- Limit decorative effects; let composition lead

## Logo/Wordmark Usage (Interim)
Until final logo is locked, use a clean typographic wordmark:
- **Primary:** BLACK ATLAS PRODUCTIONS (all caps)
- Keep minimum padding around mark
- Use black/on light or off-white/on black only
- Accent color for key punctuation lines or CTA accents only

## Imagery Direction
- Mood: nocturnal, tactile, cinematic
- Subjects: performers in motion, backstage ritual, crowd intensity
- Lighting: directional, contrast-forward
- Avoid overly glossy stock-photo aesthetics

## Messaging Stack
### Short line (social/deck)
**Ritual, risk, and remarkable craft.**

### Medium boilerplate (~50 words)
Black Atlas Productions is a Wichita-based independent production company creating bold live, stage, and screen work with mythic edge and editorial precision. We build high-impact experiences that expand paid opportunities for local artists and leave audiences inspired, changed, and moved.

### Long boilerplate (~110 words)
Black Atlas Productions is an independent Wichita-based production company focused on bold, multidisciplinary work across live events, stage, and film. Our creative identity combines raw independent energy with mythic storytelling and fashion-forward visual craft. Through programs like Black Atlas Live, we build repeatable production systems that create paid opportunities for local performers and crew while delivering culturally resonant audience experiences. We prioritize artistic integrity, technical discipline, and measurable community impact. Every project is designed to strengthen long-term infrastructure—through partnerships, documentation, and sustainable growth—so that Black Atlas can become a durable platform for original work in Wichita and beyond.
