# Personal Brand Launch Brief (v1)

Date: 2026-02-28
Owner: Jordan + Aden
Status: Draft v1

## 1) Brand Objective
Build a personal brand centered on **usefulness, credibility, and creative leadership** across performance + production + practical execution.

## 2) Positioning Statement
Jordan helps creative professionals and operators turn ideas into finished, audience-ready work through disciplined creative process, performance insight, and execution systems.

## 3) Core Audience
1. Independent artists and performers
2. Emerging producers/creative directors
3. Small business owners/creatives who need better execution systems

## 4) Value Pillars (Content + Offers)
1. **Creative Craft**
   - performance development, repertoire strategy, artistic decisions
2. **Production Execution**
   - planning, staging, run-of-show, operational discipline
3. **Systems & Momentum**
   - workflows, routines, AI-assisted planning, finishing work consistently

## 5) Signature Themes
- “From idea to stage-ready outcome”
- “Art + execution, not one or the other”
- “Structure that supports creativity”

## 6) Channel Strategy (MVP)
- **Primary:** LinkedIn + Instagram
- **Secondary:** YouTube (long-form process breakdowns)
- **Archive/home base:** website/blog later

## 7) Weekly Content Cadence (MVP)
- **2 short posts** (insight/process)
- **1 proof post** (case/example/before-after)
- **1 personal perspective post** (voice, story, lessons)

## 8) Offer Alignment (brand-to-business)
- Discovery/strategy call
- Creative direction intensive
- Production execution package

## 9) 30-Day Launch Plan

### Week 1
- Finalize bio + profile copy + visual baseline
- Write 10 post prompts mapped to value pillars

### Week 2
- Publish first 3 posts
- Start DM/reconnect outreach to warm network

### Week 3
- Publish 3 more posts including one proof/case
- Launch simple call booking CTA

### Week 4
- Publish 3 more posts
- Review analytics + identify top-performing content themes

## 10) Success Metrics (first 30 days)
- 8–12 published posts
- 10+ qualified conversations
- 2+ discovery calls booked
- 1+ paid engagement attributable to brand activity

## 11) Immediate Next Actions
1. Finalize preferred public name format + headline
2. Create 10-post idea bank in one doc
3. Draft first 3 launch posts
4. Define CTA language and booking flow
