Launch inputs you gave

  • ICP: small PM firms (US)
  • Motion: hybrid (network + cold outreach)
  • Positioning: “PM software that happens to have AI”
  • Assets requested: one-pager, pitch deck outline, demo script, website copy, email sequences

Missing: target price point, launch date, success metrics. This plan includes defaults you can override.

1) Goals + success metrics (recommended defaults)

  • Beta (30 days): 3–5 firms in active use; weekly feedback; 1–2 paid conversions.
  • Launch month (30 days): 20 qualified leads, 10 demos, 3–5 new paying customers, 500–1,500 doors onboarded.
  • Product KPIs: time to complete inspection; time from inspection to approved scope; estimate edit rate; support tickets/door/month; churn signals.

2) Positioning + messaging

Positioning statement: A modern PM suite for small firms that makes inspections and maintenance radically faster and clearer—with optional AI to draft scopes/estimates and explain uncertainty.

Core promises

  • Operational clarity: every inspection finding becomes a trackable action.
  • Faster maintenance decisions: scope + estimate range produced in minutes, not hours.
  • Owner confidence: clear explanations, assumptions, and audit trails.

3) Offer design (how we sell the first customers)

  • Founding customer program: discounted rate for 6–12 months + white-glove onboarding + roadmap influence.
  • Risk reversal: 30–60 day pilot with clear success criteria.
  • Onboarding deliverable: import properties/units/tenants + configure messaging + run first inspection workflow.

4) Channel plan (local network + cold outreach)

Local network

  • Create a short list: property managers, brokers, investor meetups, contractor networks.
  • Ask for 15-min discovery calls; convert to demo if pain is clear.

Cold outreach

  • Build list of PM firms in your state/region (then expand).
  • Outreach sequence: email + LinkedIn + phone (where appropriate).
  • CTA: “Want to see inspections → estimate in 5 minutes?”
