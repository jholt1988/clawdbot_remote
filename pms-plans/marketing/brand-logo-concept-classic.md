### **Logo Prompt: Classic & Direct (Concept 2)**

**Objective:** Create a logo for "Keyring PM," a property management software company. The design should feel established, trustworthy, and elegant, emphasizing the brand's reliability.

**Core Elements:**
*   **Logotype / Integrated Mark:** The primary feature is the typography. The logo is the name itself, with a clever twist.
    *   The letter **"K"** in "Keyring" is stylized to look like a classic, ornate **skeleton key**.
    *   The vertical stem of the "K" forms the shaft of the key.
    *   The upper and lower arms of the "K" should be curved or shaped to form the **bow (the handle)** of the key.
    *   A subtle, minimalist key **bit** should extend from the bottom of the stem.
*   **Typography:** The text "Keyring PM" should be set in a professional, elegant serif font (similar in feel to Garamond, Playfair Display, or Lora).
    *   "Keyring" is the main focus.
    *   "PM" should be in the same font but a lighter weight, perhaps in small caps, placed neatly next to "Keyring."

**Color Palette:**
*   (Consistent with Concept 1 for direct comparison)
*   **Primary Color:** A deep, trustworthy navy blue (`#0A2540`).
*   **Accent Color:** A warm, golden yellow (`#FFC107`). This could be used for the stylized "K" to make it stand out.
*   **Background:** The logo should be presented on a clean, off-white background (`#F6F9FC`).
