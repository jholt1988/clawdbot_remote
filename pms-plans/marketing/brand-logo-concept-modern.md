### **Logo Prompt: Modern & Abstract (Concept 1)**

**Objective:** Create a logo for "Keyring PM," a property management software company. The design should be modern, minimalist, and trustworthy, reflecting the brand pillars of security, simplicity, and centrality.

**Core Elements:**
*   **Logomark:** An abstract, geometric icon that cleverly combines the shape of a modern **key** with the outline of a **house**.
    *   The "head" or "bow" of the key should form the roofline of the house.
    *   The "blade" or "bit" of the key should be stylized to represent a door or the foundation of the house.
    *   The design must be made of clean, sharp lines. No curves or overly complex shapes.
*   **Logotype:** The text "Keyring PM" set in a clean, professional sans-serif font (similar in feel to Montserrat, Poppins, or Lato).
    *   "Keyring" should be in a medium or semi-bold weight.
    *   "PM" should be in a lighter weight and placed subtly next to "Keyring," either on the same baseline or slightly smaller.

**Color Palette:**
*   **Primary Color:** A deep, trustworthy navy blue (`#0A2540`).
*   **Accent Color:** A warm, golden yellow (`#FFC107`) to be used sparingly, perhaps in a small element of the logomark to represent the "spark" of AI insight.
*   **Background:** The logo should be presented on a clean, off-white background (`#F6F9FC`).
