### **Product Hunt Launch - Creative Briefs**

**Overall Vibe:** Clean, professional, modern, and trustworthy. The focus should be on clarity and demonstrating the value proposition quickly.

---

#### **1. Main Gallery Image / Hero Image**

*   **Purpose:** To grab attention and convey the core "problem/solution" in a single, compelling visual.
*   **Visual Concept:** A split-screen image similar to the blog post hero, but more dynamic for a Product Hunt audience.
    *   **Left Side:** A slightly desaturated photo of a cluttered clipboard, a calculator, and scattered paper invoices on a desk. A faint, worried-looking face of a property manager could be subtly reflected.
    *   **Right Side:** A vibrant, clean shot of a tablet displaying the PMS "AI Inspection Report." The screen is the hero, showing a clear work plan, cost estimates, and green "confidence" badges. A hand is confidently holding the tablet.
*   **Text Overlay:** "Stop Guessing. Start Predicting. AI-powered maintenance for property managers."
*   **Style Notes:** High-contrast, photorealistic. The "after" side should be brighter and more appealing.

---

#### **2. Gallery Image: The AI Inspection "Aha! Moment"**

*   **Purpose:** To showcase the final output of the core AI feature.
*   **Visual Concept:** A clean, focused screenshot of the "Routine Inspection Report" UI from the demo. The most important elements should be highlighted with subtle glows or callout boxes.
*   **Key Elements to Highlight:**
    1.  The title: "AI-Generated Work Plan"
    2.  A line item: "Replace Kitchen Faucet (High Priority)"
    3.  The cost estimate: "$250 - $350"
    4.  The "Explainability" link/button: "Why?"
*   **Text Overlay (at the top):** "Turn Inspections into Actionable Data"
*   **Style Notes:** Use a mock browser frame to make it feel like a real product screenshot.

---

#### **3. Gallery Image: Zero-Friction Stripe Onboarding**

*   **Purpose:** To communicate the speed and simplicity of getting paid.
*   **Visual Concept:** A simple, 3-step graphic flowchart.
    1.  **Step 1:** Icon of a "Connect" button with the Stripe logo. Label: "Click to Connect"
    2.  **Step 2:** An arrow points to a simplified image of the Stripe Express onboarding modal. Label: "Secure Stripe Onboarding"
    3.  **Step 3:** An arrow points to an icon of a bank deposit or dollar sign. Label: "Start Collecting Rent"
*   **Text Overlay (at the top):** "Get Paid on Day One."
*   **Style Notes:** Use brand colors and clean, universally understood icons. Keep it minimal.

---

#### **4. Gallery Image: Dashboards (PM & Owner)**

*   **Purpose:** To show the high-level view for both the manager and the investor.
*   **Visual Concept:** A composite image showing two screens.
    *   **Foreground:** A crisp screenshot of the main Property Manager dashboard, showing key metrics like "Occupancy," "Pending Tasks," and a property list.
    *   **Background (slightly smaller, angled):** A screenshot of the simpler, read-only Owner Portal, emphasizing transparency.
*   **Text Overlay:** "Manage Your Portfolio. Keep Owners Informed."
*   **Style Notes:** Use subtle shadows to create depth between the two dashboard images.
